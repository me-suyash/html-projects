Destinations

Paris, France

The city of lights! I explored the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and enjoyed French cafes.

Things to Do: Eiffel Tower, Seine River Cruise, Louvre Museum.

Food: Croissants, Baguettes, French Pastries.

Best Time to Visit: April to June, September to October.

Tokyo, Japan

A perfect blend of modern life and tradition. The food, temples, and neon streets were unforgettable.

Things to Do: Visit Shibuya Crossing, Senso-ji Temple, and Akihabara.

Food: Sushi, Ramen, Takoyaki.

Best Time to Visit: March to May, October to November.

Goa, India

Beautiful beaches, amazing sunsets, and peaceful vibes. Perfect for relaxation.

Things to Do: Beaches, Water Sports, Fort Aguada.

Food: Goan Fish Curry, Prawn Balchão, Bebinca.

Best Time to Visit: November to February.
